# SixPath

<img src="static/logo1.png" width="40%" alt="SixPaths Logo"/>

SixPath is an app for your network visualization. It aims to help the categortization of your contacts into different sectors, companies and simplify the search of contacts on your areas of interest. Furthermore, it provides the ability to save refferals so that you know which person reffered you at which company and be able to better remember your next steps on your job search journey
